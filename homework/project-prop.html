Project Proposal

Summary

The purpose of the Site is to inform people about Japnese Otaku culture. This website will provide news on recent content like manga and anime. I will be getting this news from various anime news websites. There will also be a section for newcomers to find suggestions and better understand this culture. This section will also include places where they can find articles and summaries of anime.

5 pages to include

  1. Home Page this will display updates and an overview of the website.
  2. Anime This will include new on anime releases, current seasons ongoing anime and links to sites that provide anime streaming
  3. Manga/WebtoonsThis will inclue informaation on Manga. Similar to the anime tab.
  4. Games This will have 2 sections. First, will be games that are originating from japan. Second, games that People like me like to to play. This will show stats and links to such games.
  5. ReccomendationsThis is where those who want to get started wtih these medias can get suggestions of what to watch based on their interests.

Target Audience

The target audience will be to those who are currently into the Otaku Culture and want to help others gain interest and those who have interest in learning more about the Otaku culture.
